Research

May 2012 - present : Charaterization and simulation of electrical resistivity of carbon composites under lightning strike

  • Implement numerical and analytical model describing electrical conduction mechanisms of carbon composites under various electric field/current density
  • Develop experimental charaterization methods for various specimen types including single carbon fiber, carbon fiber tow, single-ply and multi-ply composites

Sept. 2011 - May 2012 : Optimal Distribution of Materials for Better Thermal Conductivity of Composite Materials

  • Characterize thermal conductivity of composites with various layups and material configurations.
  • Develop thermal conduction model for composites using Finite Element Method (FEM)
  • Implement genetic algorithm to investigate the optimal distribution of material to get better thermal performance
